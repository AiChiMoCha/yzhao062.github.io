Research. My research accelerates and automates unsupervised ML: (1) how to speed up large-scale learning tasks with ML systems and (2) how to automate unsupervised ML model selection and hyperparameter optimization. I build AI/ML applications in finance, healthcare, and security.

  • Keywords: unsupervised ML, ML systems, Automated ML, anomaly and outlier detection.

Open-source ML. I have led more than 10 ML open-source initiatives, receiving 16,000 GitHub stars (top 0.002%: ranked 800 out of 40M GitHub users) and >15M downloads.

Ph.D. time. I got my Ph.D. in 4 years at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). At CMU, I worked with Prof. Leman Akoglu, Prof. Zhihao Jia, and Prof. George H. Chen. I was a member of Catalyst. I collaborated with Prof. Jure Leskovec at Stanford and Prof. Philip S. Yu at UIC.
